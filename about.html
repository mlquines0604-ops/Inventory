About BorrowSmart

Pioneering intelligent asset management solutions for forward-thinking organizations worldwide

Our Mission

At BorrowSmart, we believe that managing workplace assets shouldn't be complicated. Founded in 2021, we set out to create a solution that combines simplicity with powerful functionality. Our platform eliminates the friction of traditional asset tracking, replacing spreadsheets and paper logs with an intelligent, real-time system.

Today, we serve over 200 organizations across 15 countries, helping them save thousands of hours annually while reducing asset loss by up to 85%. Our commitment to innovation drives us to continuously enhance our platform with AI-powered insights and seamless integrations.
